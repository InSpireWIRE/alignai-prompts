About {{ category.name }} AI Prompts

What are {{ category.name | lower }} AI prompts?

{{ category.name }} AI prompts are pre-written instructions that tell an AI model exactly how to handle common {{ category.name | lower }} tasks for a small business. Each prompt in this category has been tested on Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini and Copilot, so you can paste it straight into the model you already use.

How do I use a prompt from this page?

Click any prompt card, copy the full text for your chosen AI model, paste it into Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini or Copilot, then replace the bracketed placeholders with your business details. The prompt is designed to run as-is on the free tier of each model.

How often are {{ category.name | lower }} prompts updated?

Every prompt is re-verified monthly and rebuilt whenever a major AI model changes. The last verified month is shown on the prompt page for each model variant.
