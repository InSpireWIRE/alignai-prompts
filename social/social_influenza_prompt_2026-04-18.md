# PROMPT — 5-Panel Cartoon: SOCIAL INFLUENZA (with PSA close)

Paste the block below into Claude, ChatGPT, or any capable LLM. Output is a cartoon script ready to hand to an illustrator, or to paste into Midjourney/DALL-E with minor reformatting.

---

```
You are writing a 5-panel social satire cartoon titled SOCIAL INFLUENZA.

TOPIC: The AI-course-seller epidemic. Specifically: how fast a first-time AI tool user pivots from user to self-declared expert to course seller.

AUDIENCE: Small-business operators and real builders who are exhausted by the AI-influencer economy on LinkedIn, X, and Substack.

TONE:
- Deadpan. Observed, not ranted.
- Specific enough that the reader recognizes someone from their own feed.
- The pros of the outbreak (knowledge democratization, first-time teachers getting a shot) live quietly in the setup panels. They are part of the joke, not a disclaimer.
- No punchline drumroll. No em-dashes. No rhetorical tricolons ("faster, cheaper, better"). No "let me think about this" preamble.

STRUCTURE:

Panels 1 through 4: follow one unnamed character through the Social Influenza arc. Each panel = a time stamp + a one-sentence visual description + a caption of 10 words or fewer.

- Panel 1: discovery. First-time AI tool user. Sympathetic framing. Morning light.
- Panel 2: symptom onset. Ring light, tripod, new LinkedIn bio containing the phrase "helping founders 10x with AI."
- Panel 3: infection. Posting a LinkedIn carousel titled "3 MISTAKES YOU'RE MAKING WITH AI."
- Panel 4: spread. Stripe dashboard showing a cohort waitlist. Comments flooded with fire emojis.

Panel 5: a PUBLIC HEALTH ADVISORY in the style of an official CDC-style bulletin. Use the exact section labels below. Keep the voice clinical and deadpan.

PANEL 5 OUTPUT FORMAT:

📢 PUBLIC HEALTH ADVISORY
CONDITION: SOCIAL INFLUENZA

[one-sentence definition of the disorder]

SYMPTOMS:
- [5 to 7 specific, observed behaviors. Each a single line. Examples of the right register: "Launching a cohort about a tool you downloaded last Tuesday." "Acute, uncontrolled use of the phrase 'let that sink in.'" "Hosting a Twitter Space you are the only guest on."]

TRANSMISSION: [one line. Mention algorithmic amplification, FOMO, and Stripe accounts.]

INCUBATION: [one line. A number of days from first viral post.]

TREATMENT: [one line. Deadpan. Reference spontaneous remission and relapse.]

PREVENTION: [one line. Read what customers said after six months, not what an influencer said on day three.]

CLOSING LINE: [one line. "Stay masked." or a variant in the same register.]

VISUAL DIRECTION (for the illustrator, include as a separate block at the end):
- Flat 2D, muted palette, slightly overlit to evoke LinkedIn sterility.
- Panels 1 through 4 share a visual language.
- Panel 5 shifts: white background, clinical sans-serif, red headline bar, small medical-cross iconography top-left.
- Captions in monospace. Time stamps smaller.

CONSTRAINTS:
- Never name the character.
- Captions 10 words or fewer (Panel 5 follows the bulletin format instead).
- No em-dashes.
- No disclaimers or "on the other hand" softeners. The PSA is the argument.
```

---

## How to use

1. Paste the block between the triple backticks into Claude.
2. You'll get a formatted cartoon script + a panel-5 PSA + visual direction block.
3. Hand the visual direction to your illustrator (or reformat panels 1–4 as individual Midjourney prompts).
4. Panel 5 can also be lifted and used standalone as a LinkedIn or Reddit post closer.
